TSB Combo-fest

(Hosted by zqil & sai)

Flashiness

What we look for: How visually impressive and exciting the combo is. This means a good number of varied and cool transitions and techs. Flashy combos are fast-paced, stunning, and really satisfying to watch.

Cleanness

What we look for: How precise and well-executed the combo is. We want to see combos done perfectly, without any wasted or pointless movements. Every action should have a clear reason, avoiding unnecessary "fluff".

Flow

What we look for: How smoothly the combo moves from one action to the next. Good-flowing combos connect naturally without awkward stops, or pauses. It should feel continuous.

Creativity & Originality

What we look for: How new and unique the combo is. We want to see combos that aren't commonly seen or rely only on widely known techs. High scores go to fresh ideas, unexpected ways to use moves, or new takes on established techniques. Show us something new that pushes the limits of what's expected in TSB.
